Bloodbound Harvest

Itch io page

Github Repo

Welcome, one and all, to the most horrible thrilling night of your life!

The spooky season is here, and the undead have risen for some hauntingly good fun. It's harvest time,
so grab your gear, get ready to collect treasures, and level up as you face some lively ghouls and ghostly surprises.
Every choice counts, so choose wisely and see how far you can go. Let the adventure begin!


The project was created in a team of 2 in a weekend with the theme of "no end" during the halloween weekend.

This project was created in Unity 6
