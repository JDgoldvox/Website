Cat-Shot

Itch io page

"Step into the arena of cat-shot, a lightning-fast showdown where two cats battle it out to see who is the kitty with the sharpest claws. There's no fancy gear, it's just you and your trusty paintball gun!"

The project was created in a team of 4 over 1 week. This was my first time using unreal engine and first ever game Jam. The theme of this game Jam was infinite space.

Tools used are using Unreal Engine 5, git lfs and github.
