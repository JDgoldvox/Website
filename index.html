What is Vox Studios?

Vox Studios is a studio that I set up in 2022 to start crafting the world’s most interactive games. It has a friendly atmosphere where people can come in and help create their dream games. We are passionate about what we do, and we’re experts in our craft. We listen to our fans carefully and put them first. We expand in confidence knowing we are doing our part for the world to be a better place. We are just a couple of people who love video games. Lastly, we are a big community of far-away friends.
